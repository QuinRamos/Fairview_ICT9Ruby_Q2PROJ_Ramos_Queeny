Breakfast Meals Ingredients Instructions

Lugaw

Ingredients:

. 1 tbsp cooking oil

. 1 piece chopped onion

. 1 piece sliced ginger

. ¼ cup of sticky rice

. 3 cups of water

. 1 piece chicken cube

. 2 pieces of boiled eggs

Instructions:

1. Pour water in a cooking pot and boil.

2. Put the rice in the boiling water and cook until it turns thick.

3. Add the salt and chicken cube and stir well.

4. Top with the eggs and chopped/sliced ingredients.

Champorado

Ingredients:

. 1 cup of glutinous sweet rice

. 2 cups of light coconut milk

. ½ cup of cocoa powder

. 1 cup of white sugar

. 1 teaspoon of salt

. 1 cup of thick coconut milk

Instructions:

1. Combine rice and light coconut milk in a large pot and boil for 10 minutes.

2. Add the cocoa powder, sugar, and salt into the rice mix and cover the pot to cook for 10 more minutes.

3. When already thickened, pour the thick coconut milk then stir to combine.

Sinangag

Ingredients:

. 2 cups of uncooked jasmine rice

. ½ cup of water

. 15 ounces of unsweetened coconut cream

. 2 teaspoons of ground turmeric

. ¼ teaspoon of salt

. 3 tablespoons of coconut oil

. ¼ cup of finely chopped garlic

. 2 tablespoons of fish sauce

Instructions:

1. Rinse and drain rice.

2. Combine the rice, water, coconut cream, turmeric, and sault in a saucepan then.

3. Boil then simmer for about 20 minutes until all the liquid is absorbed.

4. Once the rice is cooked, transfer it to a pan and add garlic, fish sauce, eggs (opt.), and vegetables of your choice (opt.).

5. Cook until lightly brown and crisp.



Lunch Meals Ingredients Instructions

Pancit Bihon

Ingredients:

. 1 pack of pancit bihon (rice noodles)

. 2 tablespoons of olive oil

. 1 chopped onion

. 2 cloves of minced garlic

. 1 cup of chopped cooked chicken

. Salt and ground black pepper

. 2 tablespoons of soy sauce

. 1 small shredded head cabbage

. 2 stalks of sliced celery

. 1 whole sliced carrot

. 2 cups of water



Instructions:

1. Soak bihon in a bowl of warm water until soft for about 5 minutes then drain.

2. Heat oil and saute the onion and garlic for about 5 minutes.

3. Add the chicken, salt, and pepper then stir well until combined.

4. Add the soy sauce, cabbage, celery, carrot, and water then boil until the vegetables soften.

5. Transfer and mix the chicken and vegetable mix into a different bowl then add the bihon in the remaining liquid.

6. Let sit until cooked well then combine the ingredients mix with the pancit bihon.

Beef Salpicao

Ingredients:

. ½ lbs. of cubed beef tenderloin

. 1 head garlic and onion

. 1 teaspoon of salt

. 3 tablespoons of butter

. ¼ cup olive oil

. ½ teaspoon of ground black pepper

. 5 tablespoons of Worcestershire Sauce

. 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce

Instructions:

1. Cook garlic in butter and oil until lightly brown and aromatic.

2. Add the seasoned ribeye and cook until seared.

3. Add the sauce mixture and stir to coat the meat evenly.

4. Top with the chopped onions and fried garlic bits.

Lumpiang Shanghai

Ingredients:

. 1 pound of ground pork

. 1 pound of ground beef

. 1 chopped medium onion

. 1 grated carrot

. ¼ cup of soy sauce

. 2 tablespoons of salt

. ½ tablespoons of garlic powder

. ½ teaspoons of black pepper

. 1 pack of spring roll wrappers

. ¼ cup water

. ½ quarts of oil

Instructions:

1. Combine the ground pork, ground beef, onion, and carrot in a large bowl then mix well.

2. Gradually blend in the soy sauce, salt, garlic powder, and pepper until incorporated.

3. Lay the wrapper and place 2 tablespoons of the filling in a line.

4. Moisten the edges with water and fold then roll; repeat.

5. Fry the lumpia in hot oil until golden brown.

Steamed Bangus

Ingredients:

. Whole bangus fish

. 2 small chopped tomatoes

. 1 chopped medium onion

. 1 clove of garlic

. 5 tablespoons of soy sauce

. Pepper

Instructions:

1. Mince the garlic and fry in a pan.

2. Open the bangus and place in the tomatoes and onions, and drizzle the soy sauce and pepper.

3. Wrap the bangus is banana leaves of aluminum foil and steam for around 15 - 20 minutes.

Chop Suey

Ingredients:

. ¼ cup of shortening

. ½ cups of diced pork loin

. 1 cup of diced onion

. 1 cup of diced celery

. 1 cup of hot water

. 1 teaspoon of salt

. ⅛ teaspoons of ground black pepper

. 1 can of drained and rinswed bean sprouts

. ⅓ cup of cold water

. 2 tablespoons of cornstarch

. 2 teaspoons of soy sauce

. 1 teaspoon of white sugar

Instructions:

1. Heat shortening over medium heat the add the pork and sear until it turns white.

2. Add onion and sauté for 5 minutes.

3. Stir in the celery, water, salt, and pepper then simmer for 5 minutes.

4. Add the sprouts then bring the mixture to a boil.

5. Combine cold water, cornstarch, soy sauce, and sugar into a small bowl then combine with the skillet mixture.

6. Cook until thickened.

Desserts Ingredients Instructions

Leche Flan

Ingredients:

. 10 pieces of eggs

. 1 can of condensed milk

. 1 cup of fresh milk or evaporated milk

. 1 cup of granulated sugar

. 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions:

1. Seperate all the egg yolks from the egg whites the beat together only all the yolks.

2. Add the milk and vanilla extract then mix thoroughly.

3. Put the mold (llanera) on top of a stove and heat.

4. Put in the sugar in the mold until caramelized then spread.

5. Pour the egg yolk mixture into the mold.

6. Cover with aluminum foil and steam for 30 - 35 minutes.

7. After, let cool then refrigerate.

Bibingka

Ingredients:

. 1 cup of rice flour

. 1/8 teaspoon of salt

. 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder

. 3 tablespoons of butter

. 1/2 cup of granulated sugar

. 1 cup of coconut milk

. 1/4 cup of fresh milk

. 1 piece of sliced salted duck egg

. 1/2 cup of grated cheese

. 3 pieces of raw eggs

. 1/4 cup of grated coconut

. Pre-cut banana leaf

Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit. 2. Combine the rice flour, baking powder, and salt then mix well.

3. Cream the butter then gradually put in the sugar while whisking.

4. Add the eggs then whisk until every ingredient is well incorporated.

5. Pour in coconut milk and fresh milk then whisk.

6. Pour the mixtue on the pan with pre-cut banana leaves.

7. Top with the eggs, cheese, and butter then bake for 30 minutes.

Maja Blanca

Ingredients:

. 4 cups of coconut milk

. 3/4 cups ofcornstarch

. 14 ounces of condensed milk

. 3/4 cups of fresh milk

. 3/4 cups of granulated sugar

. 15 ounces of whole sweet kernel corn

. 5 tablespoons of toasted grated coconut br>

Instructions:

1. Pour the coconut milk in a pot and bring to a boil.

2. Add the sugar, milk, and kernel corn and stir well then simmer for 8 minutes.

3. Combine the milk and cornstarch then stir thoroughly while heating until thickened.

4. Pour the mixture in a serving tray then flatten the top.

5. Let it cool down then refigerate for at least 1 hour.

6. Garnish with toasted grated coconut.
